About the project

Why GameVault exists

Who We Are

GameVault is a small database of video games, built to practice organizing real information — titles, genres, platforms, release dates, ratings and system requirements — into something people can actually search through and click into.

Our Mission

To catalog a wide spread of great games across genres and platforms, and give each one a proper page: real poster art, accurate stats, and honest requirements — not just a list of names.

Why a Database, Not a List?

  • Search and filter instead of scrolling forever
  • Every title gets its own dedicated page and URL
  • Genres are browsable, not just labels
  • Data lives in one place, so it stays consistent
